Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup of butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup of milk

Directions:
1. Put some butter into the pan
2. Crack eggs and mix it with milk in a bowl
3. Put the egg mixture in the pan
4. Sprinkle the salt
5. Shape the solidifying mixture into an oval shape that is pushed to the end of the pan
